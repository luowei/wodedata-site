MyWallPaper Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 3, 2025

Last Updated: October 3, 2025

Version: 1.0

Introduction

Welcome to MyWallPaper. We understand the importance of your personal information and are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Favorites data is stored locally on your device when you favorite images. This may include image IDs, favorite time, and category tags. This data is not uploaded to our servers.

Search keywords may be temporarily stored in device memory to support in-app search. Search history is not permanently saved or uploaded by us.

Automatically Collected Information

The app may process device model, operating system version, app version, device language, app usage duration, page visits, feature usage frequency, crash logs, and performance diagnostics to keep the app working properly and improve stability.

When the app loads images from Unsplash, network requests are sent to Unsplash. Unsplash may log request information such as IP address and device type under its own privacy policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, phone number, email address, or other personal identification information.
  • Precise location information.
  • Contacts, text messages, or call logs.
  • Photos or files on your device other than images you explicitly save from the app.
  • Payment information, which is handled by Apple for in-app purchases.
  • Facial recognition, fingerprint, or other biometric information.

2. Permissions

Photo Library permission is used only when you choose to save selected wallpaper images to your photo library. The app does not upload your photo library content.

Network access is used to load wallpaper content and related app resources. The current app version does not use camera functionality.

3. How We Use Information

  • Display wallpaper content from Unsplash.
  • Save your favorite image list locally on your device.
  • Provide search and image editing features.
  • Analyze app usage, fix crashes, and improve performance.
  • Display ads in the free version through Google AdMob.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information, and we do not use your information for purposes not described in this policy.

4. Storage and Security

Favorites, app settings, and image cache are stored locally on your device. Wallpaper image content is hosted by Unsplash. We do not store user data on our own servers.

Image requests use HTTPS where supported, local data is protected by iOS system security, and cache data may be cleaned automatically or manually through app settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Unsplash API

Unsplash provides wallpaper image content. Search requests are sent to Unsplash servers. We do not provide Unsplash with personally identifiable information. Unsplash Privacy Policy: https://unsplash.com/privacy

Google AdMob

Google AdMob may be used to display ads in the free version. AdMob may collect device information and ad interaction data under Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Umeng Analytics

If enabled, Umeng Analytics may collect app usage and device information for statistics and app improvement. Umeng Privacy Policy: https://www.umeng.com/page/policy

6. Advertising and Tracking

In the free version, Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers such as IDFA to display ads. You can manage tracking permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. You may also purchase the premium version to remove ads.

7. Children's Privacy

MyWallPaper is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Rights

You can view and delete favorites in the app, clear cached images in settings, uninstall the app to remove local data, and withdraw granted permissions at any time through iOS Settings.

9. International Data Transfers

Unsplash and Google services may involve cross-border data transfers. These providers are responsible for handling such transfers under their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes may be communicated in the app or by updating the Last Updated date on this page.

11. Applicable Law

For users in Mainland China, this policy is intended to comply with the Cybersecurity Law of the People's Republic of China, the Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China, and related app privacy requirements. For users in other regions, this policy follows applicable local privacy and data protection laws.
